The aim of this page is to provide the latest national protected areas statistics and global protected area maps for the 13th meeting of the Conference of the Parties (COP) to the Convention on Biological Diversity (CBD) held in Mexico in December 2016. This update has been kindly supported by the Korean National Park Service (KNPs).

The Protected Planet Report 2016 protected areas statistics were based on the April 2016 release of the World Database on Protected Areas (WDPA). See data, maps and figures (April 2016) to access all resources produced for the Protected Planet Report 2016.The WDPA is updated every month and since April 2016 there have been substantial updates in protected area statistics. As a result, changes in national and therefore global and regional statistics are expected.
