This page provides access to national statistics for every country and territory classified under the UN Environment Regions.The regions listed below are based upon UN Environment's Global Environment Outlook (GEO) process. For a detailed discussion on the use of regions for reporting and the links between the Intergovernmental Science-Policy Platform on Biodiversity and Ecosystem Services (IPBES) regional/subregional assessments please refer to the paper "Analysing biodiversity and conservation knowledge products to support regional environmental assessments" by Brooks et al, published in Nature's Scientific Data in February 2016.
