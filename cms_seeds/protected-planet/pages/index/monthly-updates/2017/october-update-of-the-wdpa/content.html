The total number of protected area records in the Octoberr 2017 release of the WDPA is 234,468 comprising of 216,026 polygons and 18,442 point, covering 245 countries and territories.

Follow us on facebook @ Protected Planet and twitter: @protectedplanet

Highlights:

Andorra: Complete update of all protected area records in Andorra, involving the conversion of two points to polygons and an update to all Ramsar sites in the country. This is the first update since 1993

Australia: Complete update of the data for Australia and its overseas territories, based on the newly released version of the Collaborative Australian Protected Area Database (CAPAD).

Haiti: Partial update to the boundaries of four sites. This is the first update since 1986. The new data was secured by meeting regional contacts at the 4th International Marine Protected Areas Congress (IMPAC4) conference in Chile in September 2017.

Spain: First ICCA (territories and areas conserved by indigenous peoples and local communities) to have gone through a nationally-determined peer-review process in order to be added to WDPA.

United Arab Emirates Partial update resulting in the integration of eight newly designated protected area records. The sites, designated in 2016, are national level designations and have added a combined area of approximately 2,500 km2 to the UAE's protected area network. Protected area records for the UAE have been updated several times in the past year, benefiting from a close working relationships with data providers.

United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland and Isle of Man: Partial update involving the addition of 45 records for privately protected areas under ownership of two UK-based charities, PlantLife and Butterfly Conservation. These records were added as a result of UNEP-WCMC's continued collaboration in the Putting Nature on the Map Project, which aims to map all privately protected areas in the UK.

WDPA Manual

WDPA User Manual: We are pleased to announce that the WDPA User Manual has been translated into French, Spanish and Russian. Additionally, the Manual has been updated with an appendix on WDPA Data Contributor Agreements (Appendix 2), and has undergone general updates and edits based on user feedback. Version 1.5 of the User Manual is now available in three languages through the geodatabase download above and via the following links:
