This GD-PAME User Manual is available for download in English, French and Spanish.

The manual has been prepared for GD-PAME data providers and users.

It provides information and guidance on:

  1. The database and its history,
  2. The GD-PAME data standards,
  3. How the evaluations are collected and managed,
  4. Type of information and format required to submit information for the GD-PAME


For any queries concerning the manual or the collation, use or processing of the database, please contact: protectedareas@unep-wcmc.org.
