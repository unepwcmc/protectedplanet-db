The total number of protected area records in the May 2017 release of the WDPA is 233,882 comprising of 215,281 polygons and 18,601 points.

Follow us on facebook @Protected Planet and twitter: @protectedplanet

Highlights:

Kiribati:Updated boundary for Phoenix Islands Protected Area.

British Virgin Islands: Complete update of national-level protected areas in the British Virgin Islands, resulting in the addition of 55 protected area records and the conversion of 25 point records to polygons.

Liberia: Update of the protected areas network for Liberia.

Ramsar Sites, Wetlands of International Importance: Continued partial update to Ramsar sites, including the addition of 21 new sites and 30 points converted to polygons,following the provision of spatial boundaries by the Ramsar Sites Information Service and updates from relevant national inventories of protected areas, for 16 countries and territories across 16 countries. These include Ramsar sites designated in 2017 in Burkina Faso, France, Guernsey, Madagascar, Myanmar and South Africa. More frequent updates of Ramsar data within the WDPA are anticipated, due to enhanced data flows between UNEP-WCMC, the Ramsar Secretariat and member states.

Natura 2000: We have received an update of the EU 28 Natura 2000 network. The new update consists of 29,482 records- which is a net update of 200 from the previous version, there have been a number of sites added (228 in all) and removed (28 in all) from several countries . This update is based upon the â€œend 2016" version of the Natura 2000 database- this is the composite version of all updates by EU Member States made throughout 2016.

Maldives: Update of boundaries for the country.

United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland: Update of attributes for a number of sites.

WDPA Manual

WDPA User Manual: We are pleased to announce that the WDPA User Manual has been translated into French and Spanish. Additionally, the Manual has been updated with an appendix on WDPA Data Contributor Agreements (Appendix 2), and has undergone general updates and edits based on user feedback. Version 1.4 of the User Manual is now available in three languages through the geodatabase download above and via the following links:

English: http://wcmc.io/WDPA_Manual

French: http://wcmc.io/WDPA_Manual_FR

Spanish: http://wcmc.io/WDPA_Manual_ES
