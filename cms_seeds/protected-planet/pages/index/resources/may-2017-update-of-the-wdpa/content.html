Follow us on facebook @Protected Planet and twitter: @protectedplanet Highlights: Kiribati:Updated boundary for Phoenix Islands Protected Area. British Virgin Islands: Complete update of national-level protected areas in the British Virgin Islands, resulting in the addition of 55 protected area records and the conversion of 25 point records to polygons. Liberia: Update of the protected areas network for Liberia. Ramsar Sites, Wetlands of International Importance: Continued partial update to Ramsar sites, including the addition of 21 new sites and 30 points converted to polygons,following the provision of spatial boundaries by the Ramsar Sites Information Service and updates from relevant national inventories of protected areas, for 16 countries and territories across 16 countries. These include Ramsar sites designated in 2017 in Burkina Faso, France, Guernsey, Madagascar, Myanmar and South Africa. More frequent updates of Ramsar data within the WDPA are anticipated, due to enhanced data flows between UNEP-WCMC, the Ramsar Secretariat and member states. Natura 2000: We have received an update of the EU 28 Natura 2000 network. The new update consists of 29,482 records- which is a net update of 200 from the previous version, there have been a number of sites added (228 in all) and removed (28 in all) from several countries .
