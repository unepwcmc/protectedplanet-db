May 2020 also sees another release of the World Database on other effective area-based conservation measures (WD-OECM). The WD-OECM can be combined with the WDPA to reveal how measures beyond protected areas contribute to conservation networks. It can be downloaded here. Follow us on Facebook @Protected Planet and Twitter: @protectedplanet Highlights: Antigua and Barbuda: This is a complete update for Antigua and Barbuda national sites. This update includes the addition of the proposed protected area Shekerley Mountains Management Area. Algeria: This is a complete update for Algeria National Sites and now all RAMSAR sites and UNESCO-MAB Biosphere reserves are in polygons. Madagascar: This is a complete update of national sites in Madagascar from the Ministry of Environment. Includes the addition of 50 sites. Mongolia: This is a complete update for Mongolia national sites. This update includes 5 new sites, 2 of which are National Parks. Niger: Three new protected areas were added to the national data including one trans-boundary Regional Park. The W Parks Regional Complex is composed of 3 National Parks in Benin, Burkina Faso and Niger. This regional park symbolises the unity and the regionality of the protected areas. Republic of Korea: This is a complete update for the Republic of Korea national sites. This update includes 54 new sites. South Africa: Complete update and validation of national sites, including 4 additions: Ingwehumbe Nature Reserve, Doringkloof Nature Reserve, Bosch Berg Nature Reserve and Marico Protected Environment. Amendments in spatial extent for Eagle Rock Private Nature Reserve and Pongola Nature Reserve. Two newly created HELCOM MPAs in Lithuania and one in Finland. OSPAR Marine protected areas: 67 new sites in United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland, 3 in Iceland, 3 in Norway, 2 in Spain, and 1 in Portugal.
