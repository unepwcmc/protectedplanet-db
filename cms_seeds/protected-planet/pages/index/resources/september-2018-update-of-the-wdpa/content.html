The total number of protected area records in the September 2018 release of the World Database on Protected Areas (WDPA) is 235,538 comprising of 218,551 polygons and 16,987 point, covering 245 countries and territories.

Follow us on Facebook @ Protected Planet and twitter: @protectedplanet

Highlights:

Algeria – Partial update, resulting in conversion of the point record for Parc National de Theniet El Had to a polygon record with a reported area of 34.24 km2.

Ethiopia – Partial update, including the addition of more detailed boundaries for the polygon records of nine existing National Parks, and the conversion of five point records to polygons.

Guatemala – Partial update, resulting in the addition of three new polygon records. These updates include a total combined area of 8 km2

Indonesia –Partial update, resulting in more accurate information on the reported area ('REP_AREA' field) for 688 polygon records.

WDPA Manual

WDPA User Manual: We are pleased to announce that the WDPA User Manual has been translated into French, Spanish, Russian and Arabic. Additionally, the Manual has been updated with an appendix on WDPA Data Contributor Agreements (Appendix 2), and has undergone general updates and edits based on user feedback. Version 1.5 of the User Manual is now available in five languages via the following links:

English: http://wcmc.io/WDPA_Manual

French: http://wcmc.io/WDPA_Manual_FR

Spanish: http://wcmc.io/WDPA_Manual_ES

Russian: http://wcmc.io/WDPA_Manual_RU

Arabic: http://wcmc.io/WDPA_Manual_AR

New: Protected Area Management Effectiveness– Country pages on Protected Planet now display information on the management effectiveness of national-level protected areas, as reported by data providers. Pages for individual protected areas now also provide information on whether the sites have Monitoring and Evaluation data in the Global Database on Protected Area Management Effectiveness (GD-PAME).
