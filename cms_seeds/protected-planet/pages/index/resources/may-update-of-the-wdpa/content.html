The total number of protected area records in the May 2016 release of the WDPA is 220,984 comprising of 202,009 polygons and 18,975 points.

Highlights:

Japan: Complete update of all national-level protected areas and those designated under international conventions and agreements, resulting in the addition of 80 new features for this country. The WDPA now includes 4,832 polygon and 90 point features for Japan.

Australia: Partial update of nationally designated sites

Timor-Leste:Partial update of nationally designated sites
