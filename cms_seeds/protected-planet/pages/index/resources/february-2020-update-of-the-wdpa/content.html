[wysiwyg content] The total number of protected area records in the February 2020 release of the World Database on Protected Areas (WDPA) is 248,303 comprising 227,053 polygons and 21,250 points, covering 245 countries and territories. February 2020 also sees the second release of the World Database on other effective area-based conservation measures (WD-OECM). The WD-OECM can be combined with the WDPA to reveal how measures beyond protected areas contribute to conservation networks. It can be downloaded here. Follow us on Facebook @Protected Planet and Twitter: @protectedplanet Highlights: Tanzania: This update comprises Forest Reserves that have changed in designation (upgraded) to Forest Nature Reserves through the following UNDP project: https://www.tz.undp.org/content/tanzania/en/home/projects/enhancing-forest-nature-reserves.html. The update also includes the removal of 4 sites that have been degazetted; Malya Stock Farm, Marenda, Puge North and Ziba.
