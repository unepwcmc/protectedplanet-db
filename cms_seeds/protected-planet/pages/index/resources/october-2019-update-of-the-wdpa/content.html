The total number of protected area records in the September 2019 release of the World Database on Protected Areas (WDPA) is 245,844 comprising 225,161 polygons and 20,683 points, covering 245 countries and territories.

Follow us on Facebook @ Protected Planet and Twitter: @protectedplanet

Highlights:

Mexico: This is a complete country update. The main highlight of this update is that 45 Voluntary Conservation Area (ADVC) have been deleted whilst another new 41 ADVCs have been added.

Europe: Part of the sequence of updates through the EEAs Common Database on Designated Areas (CDDA). This particular update only applies to the tabular information of the points data and includes updates to 7 countries.

Kuwait: A small but complete country update with now regular data providers. The country dataset is now entirely polygons. One additional site (Saad Natural Reserve) , one removal of a site (Warbah Nature Reserve) and one transition from a point to a polygon (Mubarak Al-Kabeer Reserve).
