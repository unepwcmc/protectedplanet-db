The total number of protected area records in the June 2018 release of the World Database on Protected Areas (WDPA) is 234,793 comprising of 217,795 polygons and 16,998 point, covering 245 countries and territories.

Follow us on facebook @ Protected Planet and twitter: @protectedplanet

Highlights:

Guatemala: Complete update, resulting in the addition of three new polygon records and updates to a further 150 polygon records. The existing polygon records were added in March 2018 and were updated to include new information on their status year and management authority.

Brazil: Partial update to national level protected area records, resulting in changes to the country's polygon records. This update includes the addition of 177 new records, updates to a further 1,363 records, and the removal of 84 records following their validation by the Brazilian Ministry of Environment.

WDPA Manual

WDPA User Manual: We are pleased to announce that the WDPA User Manual has been translated into French, Spanish and Russian. Additionally, the Manual has been updated with an appendix on WDPA Data Contributor Agreements (Appendix 2), and has undergone general updates and edits based on user feedback. Version 1.5 of the User Manual is now available in three languages through the geodatabase download above and via the following links:

English: http://wcmc.io/WDPA_Manual

French: http://wcmc.io/WDPA_Manual_FR

Spanish: http://wcmc.io/WDPA_Manual_ES

Russian: http://wcmc.io/WDPA_Manual_RU
