The total number of protected area records in the March 2018 release of the WDPA is 236,231 comprising of 217,825 polygons and 18,406 point, covering 245 countries and territories.

Follow us on facebook @ Protected Planet and twitter: @protectedplanet

Highlights:

Grenada: Update of one site, the Grand Anse Marine Protected Areas from Proposed to Designated. This site is part of Grenadas work on expanding its marine protected areas network and is the 4th Marine Protected Areas to be created by Grenada. For more information on this site please read this news story drafted by the Ministry of Agriculture, Lands, Forestry and Fisheries.

United Republic of Tanzania: Partial update to polygon records for 148 Forest Reserves, resulting in more accurate and complete information on management authorities and governance types in the country.

Ramsar Sites, Wetlands of International Importance: Addition of new Ramsar sites for Republic of Congo, Fiji, Italy, Morocco and Senegal with updated Rasmar sites for Ecuador. This update is part of a series of updates on Ramsar data within the WDPA, resulting from enhanced data flows between UNEP-WCMC, the Ramsar Secretariat and member states.

WDPA Manual

WDPA User Manual: We are pleased to announce that the WDPA User Manual has been translated into French, Spanish and Russian. Additionally, the Manual has been updated with an appendix on WDPA Data Contributor Agreements (Appendix 2), and has undergone general updates and edits based on user feedback.
