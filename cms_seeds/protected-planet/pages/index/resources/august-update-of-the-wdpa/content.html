The total number of protected area records in the August 2016 release of the WDPA is 230,833 comprising of 211,928 polygons and 18,905 points.

Highlights:

South Georgia and the South Sandwich Islands: Update of the national-level protected area network, including validation of the South Georgia and South Sandwich Islands Marine Protected Area record, and removal of five records identified as proposed by the data provider. Metadata for these sites is undergoing further validation, prior to reintroduction in the WDPA .

Receipt of new Natura 2000 network data set. The data set is under review by UNEP-WCMC and the updated data will be made available in the coming months.
